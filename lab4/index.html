Problem 1

  1. Gather espresso bean Nespresso cup, milk, sweet cream.
  2. Brew a single or double shot of espresso into a mug.
    3. - Insert the cup into the machine.
    - Push down on the lid.
    - Lock the lid and press the brew.
    - The espresso machine will automatically stop when complete.
  3. While the espresso is brewing, put equal parts milk and sweet cream into the Nespresso frother.(1/4 Cup each)
  4. Wait for the lid of the frother to fog up, then press the button again to stop it.
  5. Pour the milk/cream mixture into the mug slowly.
  6. Enjoy your Cappuccino!

Problem 2

  1. Register user movement, up arrow.
  2. Randomize asteroids outside of the screen and make them move only horizontally.
  3. Add score.
  4. Add a timer.
  5. If an asteroid collides with the player, reset the location.
  6. If the player reaches the top of the screen, add 1 to the score and reset the location.
  7. If timer ends time, compare player scores.
  8. Choose a player with a higher score and display it as the winner.
